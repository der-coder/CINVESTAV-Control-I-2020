\item [3.13] Find Jordan-form representations of the following matrices:

\begin{align*}
 \mathbf{A}_1 = \begin{bmatrix}
      1 & 4 & 10 \\
      0 & 2 & 0 \\
      0 & 0 & 3
     \end{bmatrix}
& \quad
\mathbf{A}_2 = \begin{bmatrix}
       0 & 1 & 0 \\
       0 & 0 & 1 \\
       -2 & -4 & -3
      \end{bmatrix}
\\
\mathbf{A}_3 = \begin{bmatrix}
       1 & 0 & -1 \\
       0 & 1 & 0 \\
       0 & 0 & 2
      \end{bmatrix}
& \quad
\mathbf{A}_4 = \begin{bmatrix}
       0 & 4 & 3\\
       0 & 20 & 16\\
       0 & -25 & -20
      \end{bmatrix}
\end{align*}

Note that all except $A_4$ can be diagonalized.


\begin{itemize}
 \item $\mathbf{A}_1$
 \item $\mathbf{A}_2$
 \item $\mathbf{A}_3$
 \item $\mathbf{A}_4$
\end{itemize}
