\item [3.12] Given

\begin{equation*}
 A = \begin{bmatrix}
      2 & 1 & 0 & 0 \\
      0 & 2 & 1 & 0 \\
      0 & 0 & 2 & 0 \\
      0 & 0 & 0 & 1
     \end{bmatrix}
\qquad
b = \begin{bmatrix}
     0 \\
     0 \\
     1 \\
     1
    \end{bmatrix}
\qquad
\bar b = \begin{bmatrix}
          1 \\
          2\\
          3\\
          1
         \end{bmatrix}
\end{equation*}

what are the representations of $A$ with respect to the basis $[b, \/ Ab, \/A^2 b,\/ A^3b]$ and the basis $[\bar b, \/ A \bar b, \/A^2 \bar b,\/ A^3 \bar b]$, respectively?
(Note that the representations are the same!)
